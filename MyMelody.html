My Melody, born on January 18th, is a white rabbit who wears a pink (or red) hood that covers her ears. She was created in 1975 by Yasuko Matsumoto. She was born in a forest called Mariland, which is where most of her friends are from.
My Melody has a younger brother named Rhythm, and her best friends are Hello Kitty, Flat, and My Sweet Piano. One of her hobbies is baking almond cakes. Her friendly rival is Kuromi.
Just like other Sanrio characters, My Melody has been a fixture on various media and stationery. Her television debut was in 1987, in the series Hello Kitty's Furry Tale Theater.
